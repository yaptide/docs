The particle source and physics parameters are configured in the **Settings** tab in the right-hand panel of the Editor.

## Particle source (beam)

![Beam settings](/docs/assets/settings/beam_settings.png)

You can define:

- **Position** and **direction** of the beam
- **Energy** and energy spread
- **Beam shape** (circular, rectangular, etc.) and divergence
- **Particle type** (protons, carbon ions, etc.)
- **Number of primaries** (how many particles to simulate)

### Configuring the beam for our example

We want to fire a 70 MeV proton beam at the collimator-phantom geometry we built in the [previous section](/docs/user-guide/geometry/).
The beam should be wider than the collimator hole (radius 2 cm) so that we can observe the collimation effect.

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="SHIELD-HIT12A">

Move the beam in front of the collimator by setting position to `(0, 0, −1.5)` cm.
Set energy to **70 MeV** with **1% energy spread** — this is enough for 1 cm of lead to stop most protons (the CSDA range in lead at 70 MeV is about 8 mm).
Set beam shape to circular with **3 cm radius**.

![Setting up beam](/docs/assets/settings/beam_adjustments.png)

Leave the remaining parameters at their defaults.

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="FLUKA">

:::note[Under development]

:::

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="Geant4">

Move the beam in front of the collimator by setting position to `(0, 0, −6)` cm.
Set energy to **70 MeV** with **1% energy spread**.
Set beam shape to circular with **3 cm radius**.

![Setting up beam](/docs/assets/settings/geant4_beam_adjustments.png)

Leave the remaining parameters at their defaults.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Physics settings

Physics settings are defined in the **Physics** area just below the beam definition.

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="SHIELD-HIT12A">

You can tune:

- **Step length** via the relative energy loss parameter — decreasing it reduces the step size for more accurate but slower tracking
- **Nuclear reactions** — toggle on/off
- **Scattering models** — choose from available models

![Physics settings](/docs/assets/settings/physics_settings.png)

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="FLUKA">

:::note[Under development]
FLUKA physics configuration details will be added once support is complete.
:::

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="Geant4">

Geant4 physics configuration through the UI is currently not supported. Default physics lists are used.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
